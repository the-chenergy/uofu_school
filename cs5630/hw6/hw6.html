The State of the State of the States

Qianlang Chen (u1172983), qianlangchen@gmail.com

At the beginning of each year, each governor lays out their policy priorities in their version of the State of the Union address, a "state of the state" address. The team at 538 constructed a text analysis of all 50 governors' 2019 state of the state speeches to see what issues were talked about the most and whether there were differences between what Democratic and Republican governors were focusing on.
